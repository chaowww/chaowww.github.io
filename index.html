Lab Introduction

I am an Associate Professor and Researcher at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, serving as a Master's and Ph.D. supervisor. My research focuses on optoelectronic integrated circuits, intelligent computing, optical sensing, optical interconnects, and on-chip system integration. As the first or corresponding author, I have published over 30 high-level academic papers in journals including Nature Photonics. I developed the world's first 100-nanometer bioparticle optoelectronic intelligent sensing platform and pioneered the concept of a million-TOPS general-purpose optical computing chip, architecture, and system. I have applied for more than 10 national invention patents and led/participated in multiple projects including the National Natural Science Foundation Major/Key Projects, the Ministry of Science and Technology Key R&D Young Scientist Project, the Ministry of Science and Technology Innovation 2030 Major Project, and the National Key R&D Program.

Research Focus

  • Optoelectronic intelligent computing chips and systems
  • Embodied perception fusion technology
  • On-chip photonic integration systems
  • Biophotonics sensing and detection
  • Optical interconnects and high-speed communication
