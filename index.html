个人与实验室简介

我是北京邮电大学副教授，研究员，硕士生和博士生导师，长期从事光电子集成电路、智能计算、光传感、光互连及片上系统集成方向研究，以第一及共同作者在Nature Photonics等发表高水平学术论文30余篇，研制世界首台百纳米生物粒子光电智能传感平台，原创性提出百万TOPS通用光学计算芯片、架构及系统，申请国家发明专利10余项，主持/参与国家自然科学基金重大/重点项目、科技部重点研发青年科学家项目、科技部科技创新2030重大项目及国家重点研发计划项目等多项。

About & Lab

I am an Associate Professor and Researcher at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) and a PhD supervisor. My research focuses on photonic/electronic integrated circuits and related topics.

招聘信息（长期）

长期招收：博士 / 硕士、博士后、实习（连续半年以上）

研究方向

光电/光子智能计算、具身感知融合与光电系统集成等。

你将得到

  • 系统指导：导师在理论与实验研究、论文写作与项目管理方面提供全方位指导；
  • 产业/项目资源：长期的产学研合作可提供实习与落地项目机会；
  • 科研支持：竞争性科研补助、设备与材料支持，以及团队福利；
  • 良好环境：实验室位于海淀校区，科研资源与交通生活便利；
  • 成长伙伴：重视团队氛围与个人发展，导师注重学术与生活的平衡。

专业要求

计算机、人工智能、微电子、电子、光学、机器人等相关理工科背景优先，欢迎有强烈科研兴趣与创新能力的同学投递简历。

Open Positions (Long-term)

We are recruiting long-term: PhD / Master students, Postdoctoral researchers, Interns (minimum continuous 6 months).

Research Directions

Edge computing, optoelectronic/photonic intelligent computing, sensing-compute integrated systems, embodied intelligence & security, etc.

What you will get

  • Personal growth: full mentorship from the PI in theory, experiments, and publications;
  • Industry connections: the PI has extensive industry collaborations and can provide internship and real-world project opportunities;
  • Research support: competitive material/subsidy support, plus snacks, holiday gifts, and team-building activities;
  • Excellent research environment: the lab is located inside Haidian campus (within Beijing's 3rd Ring), with abundant resources and convenient transportation;
  • Friendly mentorship: a young PI who cares about your life and interests — we grow together.

Requirements

Backgrounds such as Computer Science, AI, Microelectronics, Electronics, Optics, Robotics and other STEM fields are welcome. Strong research motivation and creativity are valued.
