个人与实验室简介

我是北京邮电大学副教授、研究员、博士生导师，长期从事光电子集成电路、智能计算、光传感、光互连及片上系统集成方向的研究，致力于将光子学与人工智能、边缘计算相结合，推动下一代高效智能计算与感知系统的发展。实验室欢迎有志于在理论与实验上深耕的同学加入。

About & Lab

I am an Associate Professor and Researcher at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT). My lab focuses on photonic/electronic integrated circuits, photonic intelligent computing, optical sensing, optical interconnects and on-chip system integration — combining photonics with AI and edge computing. We welcome motivated students for both theory and experiments.

招聘信息（长期）

长期招收：博士 / 硕士、博士后、实习（连续半年以上）

研究方向

边缘计算、光电计算、感算一体、具身智能与安全等（详见主页）。

你将得到

  • 自我成长：在理论、实验、论文等各个方面获得导师全方位指导；
  • 产业界关系：导师在产业界有大量合作积累，可提供丰富的实习机会和实战项目经历；
  • 科研补助：高质量物质补贴，以及零食、节日礼、团建等各种福利安排；
  • 优异的科研环境：实验室位于北京三环内海淀校区，科研资源丰富，生活交通便利；
  • 亦师亦友：年轻导师，你的生活和爱好也会被留意，一起努力成为更好的人。

专业要求

计算机、人工智能、微电子、电子、光学、机器人等理工科背景均可，只要你足够优秀就有机会加入我们！

Open Positions (Long-term)

We are recruiting long-term: PhD / Master students, Postdoctoral researchers, Interns (minimum continuous 6 months).

Research Directions

Edge computing, optoelectronic/photonic computing, sensing-compute integrated systems, embodied intelligence & security, etc. (see homepage for details).

What you will get

  • Personal growth: full mentorship from the PI in theory, experiments, and publications;
  • Industry connections: the PI has extensive industry collaborations and can provide rich internship and real-world project opportunities;
  • Research support: competitive material/subsidy support, plus snacks, holiday gifts, and team-building activities;
  • Excellent research environment: the lab is located inside Haidian campus (within Beijing's 3rd Ring), with abundant resources and convenient living/transportation;
  • Friendly mentorship: a young PI who cares about your life and interests — we grow together.

Requirements

Backgrounds such as Computer Science, AI, Microelectronics, Electronics, Optics, Robotics, and other STEM fields are welcome. If you are outstanding, you have the chance to join us!
