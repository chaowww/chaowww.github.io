About & Lab

I am an Associate Professor and Researcher at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) and a PhD supervisor. My research focuses on photonic/electronic integrated circuits and devices, photonic intelligent computing, optical sensing and interconnects, and system-on-chip integration. As first or co-first author I have published 30+ high-impact papers in venues including Nature Photonics; I led the development of the world’s first sub-100nm bio-particle optoelectronic intelligent sensing platform; I originated designs and system concepts for million-TOPS-class general-purpose optical computing chips; I have filed 10+ national invention patents and participated in multiple national-level projects (NSFC major/key programs, MOST key R&D plans, Science & Technology Innovation 2030 projects, etc.).

招聘信息（长期）

长期招收：博士 / 硕士、博士后、实习（连续半年以上）

研究方向

光电/光子智能计算、具身感知融合与光电系统集成等。

你将得到

  • 系统指导：导师在理论与实验研究、论文写作与项目管理方面提供全方位指导；
